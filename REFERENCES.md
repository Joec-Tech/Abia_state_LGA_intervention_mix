## Cost-year conversion

16. US Bureau of Labor Statistics. *Historical Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U).* https://www.bls.gov/cpi/tables/supplemental-files/historical-cpi-u-202411.pdf

## Important transferability note

Published costs differ by price year, perspective, delivery platform, geography and scale. They are reference inputs, not definitive Abia procurement prices. The model preserves source, unit and uncertainty fields so that government budget data can replace them without changing the epidemiological model.
