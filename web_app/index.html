Interpretation warning

The SARIMA forecast describes reported malaria cases under continuation of historical patterns. Because verified baseline intervention coverage is unavailable, this explorer estimates prevention potential from additional effective coverage rather than causal impact from measured current coverage. The results support scenario planning and stakeholder discussion; they should not be used alone for final procurement or budget allocation.

Historical data: January 2021–April 2026. Forecast horizon: May 2026–April 2028.
